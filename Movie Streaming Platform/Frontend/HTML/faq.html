Getting Started with CineCast

Welcome to CineCast! Below you'll find some information to get you started. If you don’t see a topic covered here, try searching for it in our Help Center. If you haven't signed up yet and would like to learn more, visit What is CineCast?

Signing in

Once you've opened the CineCast app or website, select Sign In to access your account and begin watching TV shows and movies. You can sign in on any CineCast-compatible device or on multiple compatible devices. If you experience any issues, review the troubleshooting steps at Can’t sign in to CineCast. If you don't have the CineCast app, visit How to download the CineCast app.

Creating profiles

You can create profiles for members in your household, allowing them to have their own personalized CineCast experience. Your account can have up to four individual profiles, and you can set a maturity rating level on each one. Each profile will have its own recommendations based on that profile's ratings and tastes.

Finding TV shows and movies

You can search for titles you’re interested in or browse suggestions provided by CineCast. Once you start viewing and rating titles, CineCast will show recommendations. You can also enable subtitles, captions, or alternate audio on many titles, or browse titles with your preferred subtitle or audio language.

Managing your account

You can update your account information at any time, and change your email, phone number, or membership plan by selecting the Account option within the CineCast menu. With Profile & Parental Controls, you can also adjust content controls, such as playback preferences, language, and subtitles. The articles below can help you learn how to manage your account.

Streaming on Different Devices

If you have more than one CineCast-compatible device, you can switch devices at any time. Your membership plan determines the number of screens you can watch at the same time, but it does not restrict the number of devices you can associate with your account. If you want to watch on a new or different device, sign in to CineCast on that device. You can find more information about CineCast-compatible devices or our article about downloading the CineCast app for help setting up a new device.

CineCast Gift Cards

CineCast gift cards are not currently sold in all countries. If you purchased a CineCast gift card in a different country, you can use it as long as you pay for CineCast in the same currency as the gift card.
For more information, check the availability of CineCast gift cards in your country.
Note: To redeem the gift card, you may need to add a secondary payment method.
